Fishing

There are a number of small creeks and rivers which generally flow clear, providing excellent fishing at the point they enter the lake.

For the shoreline angler, some of the best fishing is to be found around the mouths of these small streams. Most anglers however prefer to troll a lure from a boat.

Nzfishing.com is another fantastic website containing everything you need to know about fishing in the lake.

It provides information on what type of fish you are allowed to catch, how many and the size limits for the fish.

There is also an incredibly detailed pdf file containing everything you will need to know for the entire Central South Island.

This website (www.see.cam) allows you to view a webcam of the lake updated every few hours and even has a timelapse funcion. This is useful if you want to check the current weather conditions for fishing and even the conditions in the past.

AccuWeather also provides details and numbers on the weather and other conditions.
